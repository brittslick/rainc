Writer Guidelines & Strategic Notes

  • Consistency is critical: All messaging must ladder up to "Empower Business Growth" umbrella theme
  • Business Formation Report: Use as anchor proof point in all layers (becomes "source of truth")
  • Proof points to integrate: Monthly customer surveys, real founder examples, state-specific data, community testimonials
  • Avoid: Privacy/controversy language in Layers 1 & 2 (privacy ONLY used in Layer 3 Libertarian/MAGA as contrarian hook)
  • Tone progression: Layer 1 = professional/accessible, Layer 2 = substantive/credible, Layer 3 = persuasive/emotional
  • Wyoming exception: May need separate treatment due to unique characteristics
  • Reusability: Structure all content to be easily adapted into scripts, web copy, FAQs, and state pages
  • Alignment requirement: PR and government outreach must use the same consistent story
